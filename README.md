🧠 Prompt Engineering
Nimbus AI uses structured prompts to improve:

Accuracy
Consistency
Clarity
Professionalism

Example Prompt:

Act as a professional business assistant. Draft a formal and polite annual leave request email.
Audience: My direct manager
Tone:Professional and courteous
Instructions for the email:Subject
Line: Create a clear, concise subject line containing your name and the purpose of the email (e.g., Annual Leave Request).
Greeting: Use a formal and professional salutation.
Purpose: Clearly state your intention to request time off for this coming Friday. Include the exact date and confirm that you are using annual leave/PTO.
Workload (Optional but recommended): Briefly mention that you will wrap up any pending tasks or delegate urgent matters to a colleague to ensure a smooth workflow.
Closing: Express gratitude for their time and consideration. Add a professional sign-off with your name and contact information.
